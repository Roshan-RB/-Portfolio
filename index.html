About Me

I’m an AI Engineer with a background in product design, manufacturing and digitalization, focused on building practical, production-ready AI systems for real industrial challenges.

My work spans Computer Vision, 3D applications, Vision-Language Models, RAG systems, and ML pipelines used in engineering and manufacturing domains. I enjoy working on the full lifecycle of an AI solution — from understanding technical requirements to designing models, building scalable pipelines, and deploying them in real environments.

I’ve developed solutions such as Automated analysis of Technical Documents using hybrid LLM Frameworks, multimodal retrieval systems for technical document QA, prediction models for configuring complex fuel-cell test stations, OCR workflows, and end-to-end MLOps systems with MLflow, Docker, and CI/CD.

Across projects, my goal is simple: use AI to reduce manual effort, improve decision-making, and unlock new levels of efficiency in engineering processes.
